Who am I?

On the surface I am a "sorta-rican" (half Puerto Rican) girl living in Atlanta and going to school. Being a full-time student is cool and all but there's so much more to do and be in Atlanta. Keep scrolling to see the things I enjoy in life.

Highlights

Born and raised in Kennesaw, Georgia, I've been lucky enough to be exposed to a lot of unique experiences and grow my list of hobbies over time.

Bailey

My dog is my favorite thing in my life. Bailey is our three year old "pocket pit" we adopted in 2016. She's known for thinking she's better than you, having no sense of personal space, and stepping on people's toes. Literally.

Food

It's hard to live in the A and not turn into a foodie. There are so many new restaurants with unique menus and I want to try them all. I really enjoy the food scene in East Atlanta Village and having been making my way down the line of restaurants along Flat Shoals Ave. Even with all the new and exciting places to try, I'm an ATLien at heart and will take Waffle House over whatever is trending any day.

Music

Music is a pretty big part of my life as I am always listening to it. I picked up the bass in 6th grade orchestra and played on through middle and high school in orchestra, jazz band, concert band, as well as a rock band with some friends. We even played at the Masquerade! This led to a love for concert going and now I attend as many as I can.

Next concert: Rex Orange County


Binging

I spend more time than I should binge-watching Netflix or Hulu. I can't help it! The originals are too good to resist. I prefer series over movies and enjoy thrillers, anthologies, and sitcoms. Some all time favorites include: Russian Doll, Blacklist, Brooklyn 99, and Black Mirror.

Current Binge: Castle Rock (Hulu)

Dancing

As someone with Puerto Rican heritage, salsa dancing is something that I was raised on. My dad and I are regulars at SalsAtlanta's monthly parties and it's always a blast.
